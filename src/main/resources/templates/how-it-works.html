How to Upload Your Used Items

Create and Account/Login
Start by signing up on the 'Sustain n' Share' website. Provide basic information such as your name, email, and location.
Once your account is set up, log in to access the donation features.
List Your Items
Click on the 'Profile' link, then click on the 'Donate Item' button. Here, you can list the items you wish to donate. Include clear photos, detailed descriptions, and the condition of each item.
Wait for Interest
Interested users will contact you through the platform. You might receive questions about the items, so be ready to respond.
Coordinate with Recipient
Once someone expresses interest in an item, coordinate with them directly to arrange a pick-up or delivery time and location.
Update Listing
After the item has been successfully picked up or delivered, make sure to update or remove the listing from the site.

How to Obtain Used Items

Create and Account/Login
If you haven’t already, sign up on the 'Sustain n' Share' website providing basic information.
Access the platform by logging into your account.
Browse Listing
Navigate to the 'Search for Items' page. Here, you can browse through various categories of available items, such as furniture, clothes, electronics, etc.
Use the search function and filters to narrow down items based on your specific needs, location, and preferences.
Express Interest
When you find an item you need, express your interest directly through the platform. You can ask the donor questions or request more information.
Coordinate Pick-Up or Delivery
If the donor approves, arrange a time and place to pick up the item. Ensure to follow any specific instructions the donor may have.
Collect the Item
Collect the item at the agreed time and location. It’s always good practice to confirm the pick-up with the donor.
