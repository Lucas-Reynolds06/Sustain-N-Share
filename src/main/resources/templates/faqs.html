Frequently Asked Questions

Sustain n' Share is a platform that connects people who want to donate items with those in need. You can list items you wish to donate or browse through available items if you're looking for something. Our goal is to facilitate the sharing and recycling of goods to reduce waste and support our community.

You can donate a variety of items, including clothing, electronics, furniture, books, toys, and more. We ask that all items be in good, usable condition. For a detailed list of acceptable items, please visit our Donation Guidelines page.

No, Sustain n' Share is completely free to use. Our aim is to create a community-driven platform that supports sustainability without any cost barrier.

To list an item, simply create an account, click on the 'Donate Item' button, and fill in the details about the item. Include a description, photos, and your location for potential recipients to view.

Once a recipient expresses interest in your item, you can communicate directly with them through our platform to arrange pick-up or delivery.

Currently, our services are available in select areas. Please check our Location Page to see if your area is included. We are constantly expanding, so stay tuned for updates!

We encourage donors to provide clear photos and descriptions. As a recipient, you can always ask the donor questions and inspect the item upon pick-up.

If you encounter any issues, please contact us immediately through our support page. Our team is dedicated to ensuring a smooth and safe experience for all users.

By facilitating the reuse and recycling of items, we help reduce waste and the environmental impact associated with producing new goods. Our platform promotes a circular economy, extending the life of items and conserving resources.

We welcome community involvement! You can support us by donating items, sharing our platform with others, or volunteering. If you're interested in partnering with us or contributing in other ways, please visit our Get Involved page.
